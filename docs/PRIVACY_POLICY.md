# Privacy Policy — Music for Reading

**Last updated:** June 24, 2025

Music for Reading ("the App") is developed as an offline ambient music player for reading.

## Data collection

The App does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Specifically, the App:

- Does not require an account
- Does not use analytics or advertising SDKs
- Does not access the internet
- Does not read contacts, location, files, or other device data

## Permissions

The App requests only permissions needed for background music playback:

- **Foreground service (media playback)** — to keep music playing while the screen is off
- **Notifications** (Android 13+) — to show playback controls in the notification shade

## Children's privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at the same URL before the next app release.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer via the email address listed on the Google Play Store listing.
